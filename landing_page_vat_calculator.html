VAT calculator

Welcome to my VAT calculator. This calculator will help you calculate the VAT (Value added tax) for purchases/sales of physical goods or services.

Short description

This calculator will be used to calculate a VAT on a purchase. You will have to select:

  • The country of origin;
  • The final country;
  • Name of the commodity - service or physical goods;
  • Amount of purchase;
  • Any other information.
